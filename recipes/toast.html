Toast

Description

Toast is toasted bread, often topped with butter, jam or other spreads. It may look dark, but don't let that get in your head - it's not burnt, and it's simple enough to eat in bed.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Small amount of butter

Steps

  1. Place bread in toaster, and set to medium, or whatever setting you prefer.
  2. Start the toaster.
  3. When finished, remove toast, spread butter and any other topping you like, and serve!

Chocolate-covered Strawberries

Description

Chocolate-covered strawberries are an easy, tasty dessert for as many people as you have strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 3 ounces white chocolate, chopped
  • 1 pound strawberries with stems (about 20), washed and dried very well

Steps

  1. Put semisweet and white chocolate into 2 separate bowls. Fill 2 saucepans with 2 inches of water and simmer over medium heat. Turn off heat; set bowls of chocolate over water to melt. Stir until smooth.
  2. Once melted and smooth, remove chocolates from heat. Line sheet pan with parchment or waxed paper. Holding strawberry by stem, dip fruit into dark chocolate, lift an twist slightly, letting excess chocolate fall into the bowl. Set strawberry on parchment paper. Repeat with rest of strawberries, then dip fork in white chocolate and drizzle on strawberries.
  3. Wait for chocolate to set, then serve!

Pasta and Meatballs

Description

Pasta and meatballs is a classic Italian dish, which is exactly what it sounds like: pasta with meatballs. Served with tomato sauce and grated parmesan cheese, it's an easy, tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 20 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Drain.
  2. In large bowl, mix beef and egg. Roll into 16 balls.
  3. Heat oil in large pot over medium heat. Add meatballs and cook for about 10 minutes, until browned on all sides. Transfer meatballs onto plate.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes to pot, season with salt and pepper, simmer, put meatballs back into pot, and cover. Simmer 8-10mins until sauce has thickened.
  5. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
